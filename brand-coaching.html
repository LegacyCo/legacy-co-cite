The Imprint Intensive is a focused 1:1 engagement designed for founders who are done performing a version of themselves online. We go deep — into your calling, your story, your message — and build a brand framework that actually holds up when life gets hard.

What it is

Deep Identity Work

We excavate your story, your gifts, your values, and the unique mark God has placed on you — and translate all of it into a cohesive brand framework.

What it isn't

Not a Logo Package

This is not a design sprint or a social media audit. This is the foundational clarity that makes everything else click — before you invest in execution.

The outcome

Clarity That Holds

You'll walk away with a Brand Imprint — your identity foundation — that becomes the filter for every creative decision you make going forward.
