01

Information We Collect

Legacy Creative Co. gathers several categories of data to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information — Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details provided by you
  • Mobile Information — Phone numbers designated for communication and messaging purposes
  • User-Generated Content — Data from chats, forms, or submissions on our platform
  • Technical Information — IP address, browser type, and usage data collected via cookies or similar technologies

02

How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Respond to your inquiries and send relevant updates
  • Send text messages and emails related to our services
  • Personalize your experience and improve website functionality

03

Collection & Use of Mobile Phone Numbers

Mobile phone numbers will be used exclusively for communication purposes related to our services.

We do not share your mobile information with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes. Your number is used only to communicate with you directly about your engagement with Legacy Creative Co.

04

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with unaffiliated third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • To comply with applicable legal requirements or respond to lawful requests
  • To protect the security or integrity of our services
  • When providing services with trusted partners who are bound by confidentiality agreements

05

Storage of Data in CRM

All data collected via chat or website interactions is securely stored in our CRM system. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

06

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to analyze performance and improve your experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us understand how you interact with our site.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect some functionality of the site.

07

Access & Updates to Your Information

You may request access to, updates to, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at support@legacycreative.co.

We will respond to all requests within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

08

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

09

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, rectify, or request deletion of your personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 12 below.

10

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

11

This Privacy Policy is accessible at all times via a direct link in our website footer and within any messaging workflows where your mobile number or other personal information is collected.
