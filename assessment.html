The Imprint Framework

Four Archetypes.
One Is Yours.

Each archetype carries a distinct calling, a distinct voice, and a distinct way of building. The assessment will identify which of the four reflects your God-given wiring.

P

The Prophet

"Speak, for your servant is listening." — 1 Samuel 3:10

Built to name what others won't. You see the gap before anyone else does. Your brand carries a message that disrupts the status quo and calls people to something higher.

S

The Shepherd

"He tends his flock like a shepherd." — Isaiah 40:11

Built to guide, protect, and bring people home. You build community. Your brand creates a sense of belonging. People trust you because you show up consistently and carry them with you.

B

The Builder

"Unless the Lord builds the house..." — Psalm 127:1

Built to create systems, structures, and things that last. You don't just cast vision — you execute. Your brand is known for results, process, and doing the thing others only talk about.

A

The Ambassador

"We are therefore Christ's ambassadors." — 2 Cor 5:20

Built to represent and connect. You bridge worlds — translating complex ideas into accessible ones, bringing people into the room who wouldn't otherwise find each other.
