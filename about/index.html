The Person Behind the Work

Let's
Meet.

KC Clark is the Chief Curator for Legacy Co, a resource for the creative class. In addition to running a full brand development and management agency, he is a full-time artist, mentor, and coach.

His passion to see artists, brands, and non-profits thrive is rooted in a desire for individuals with unique perspectives to come alive to their calling in both life and career pursuits.

Everything built here is grounded in one conviction: clarity is the most underrated gift you can give a brand.

The Legacy Story

Where This
All Began.

Legacy Co was built out of a simple observation: too many talented founders, artists, and mission-driven organizations were showing up in the world without the language to describe what made them different.

KC spent years working at the intersection of creativity, faith, and entrepreneurship — watching brilliant people shrink their vision because they couldn't articulate it. The Brand Bridge Framework was born from that frustration.

Today, Legacy Co serves Kingdom-minded founders who want a brand that does more than look good — one that reflects who they actually are, and who they're becoming.
